A Mouse in my Pocket

Published: Wed 30 July 2014
By Terrel Shumway

In family.

tags: silly

Whenever someone in our family says "We are going to do ..." and the other person does not agree or want to participate, he asks "Do you have a mouse in your pocket? You must, because you said we."

Lately, JoyLynn has responded, "Yes, I do have a mouse in my pocket. He helps me keep track of my marbles, so I don't lose them."

Tonight, after she put on her pajamas, which don't have pockets, she said "Yes, my mouse is in my invisible pocket with my marbles." Then she starts imagining a sales pitch for someone trying to sell invisible pockets.

Last night, she told me about a creature she invented called a quadropus. It is like an octopus, but with four tentacles and four eyes evenly spaced aroud its head. I showed her a picture of Octocat, the GitHub mascot.

I love her creativity.
